☠ Links ☠

Here are some of the best Monkey Island and LucasArts adventure game resources on the web! If you'd like to exchange links, please email the webmaster.

★ Monkey Island Fan Sites

The SCUMM Bar ★
THE definitive Monkey Island fan site. Been around since 1996 and has absolutely everything: news, game info, media, trivia, easter eggs, and more. If you only visit one MI site besides this one, make it The SCUMM Bar!
The International House of Mojo
Incredible LucasArts adventure game fan site covering not just Monkey Island but ALL the classic LucasArts adventures: Day of the Tentacle, Sam & Max, Full Throttle, Grim Fandango, and more. Essential bookmarking!
World of Monkey Island
Great fan site with walkthroughs, character bios, and a lively forum community. Their MI2 walkthrough got me through the waterfall puzzle!
Monkey Island Headquarters
Another excellent MI resource with screenshots, reviews, and fan art. They run a cool monthly MI trivia quiz on their message board.
LeChuck's Lair
A site dedicated to the greatest villain in adventure gaming! Covers LeChuck's appearances across all three games. Dark background, spooky MIDI music — very atmospheric!

★ LucasArts & Adventure Games

LucasArts Official Site
The official LucasArts website. Check here for news about upcoming games and patches. They've been awfully quiet about adventure games lately though... :(
The Adventurer's Guild
Covers all adventure games, not just LucasArts. Great reviews and a huge walkthrough archive. Their forum is really active and friendly!
SCUMM Revisited
A technical site about the SCUMM engine itself. If you're interested in how these games work under the hood, this is fascinating stuff. Features tools to peek inside game data files!
Grim Fandango Network
A fan site for LucasArts' latest masterpiece, Grim Fandango. If you haven't played it yet, what are you waiting for?! Manny Calavera is almost as cool as Guybrush. Almost.

★ General Gaming

GameFAQs
The internet's largest collection of game guides, FAQs, and walkthroughs. If you're stuck in any game, chances are there's a guide here.
Adventure Gamers
News and reviews focused on the adventure game genre. Fighting the good fight to keep adventure games alive! Check out their "adventure games are NOT dead" editorial.
